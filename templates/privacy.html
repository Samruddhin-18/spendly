Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when creating an account, along with the expense entries you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the service: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expenses, and generating summaries and category breakdowns for you. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Storage

Your account details and expense data are stored securely in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data and expense records within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers, such as hosting and database services, to operate the app. These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
